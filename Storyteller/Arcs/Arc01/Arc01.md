# Arc01 - The Missing Neonate<br><br>⚠️ STORYTELLER FILE ⚠️<br><br>DO NOT READ THIS FILE AS A PLAYER.<br><br>This document contains hidden information intended exclusively for Storyteller use.<br><br>---<br><br># Arc Summary<br><br>Arc01 serves as the introduction to Saint Veyre.<br><br>Its purpose is to:<br><br>- Introduce the city.<br>- Introduce major factions.<br>- Introduce several recurring NPCs.<br>- Demonstrate how influence operates within Saint Veyre.<br>- Establish Alec's first reputation.<br>- Provide opportunities rather than obligations.<br>- Lay groundwork for future arcs.<br><br>The mystery is intentionally local and limited in scope.<br><br>Saint Veyre itself is the true focus of the arc.<br><br>---<br><br># Hook<br><br>A young Kindred named Julien Armand has disappeared.<br><br>The disappearance is not publicly treated as a crisis.<br><br>Julien was not particularly important.<br><br>He possessed little status, little influence, and few allies.<br><br>Nevertheless, several individuals would prefer to know what happened.<br><br>Rumors begin circulating.<br><br>Stories conflict.<br><br>Nobody appears particularly motivated to investigate.<br><br>For an ambitious newcomer, however, information represents opportunity.<br><br>---<br><br># Initial Situation<br><br>Alec arrives in Saint Veyre seeking:<br><br>- Independence.<br>- Opportunity.<br>- Recognition.<br>- A place within the city's social hierarchy.<br><br>During her first weeks she becomes aware of Julien's disappearance through ordinary conversation, rumor, and social encounters.<br><br>Different individuals possess different pieces of information.<br><br>Nobody possesses the entire picture.<br><br>---<br><br># Truth<br><br>Julien Armand is dead.<br><br>---<br><br># Victim<br><br>## Julien Armand<br><br>Clan: Toreador<br><br>Apparent Age: Mid-Twenties<br><br>Status: Neonate<br><br>Julien possessed more confidence than wisdom.<br><br>He became convinced he had uncovered valuable information that could elevate his status.<br><br>Unfortunately, he was correct.<br><br>---<br><br># What Actually Happened<br><br>Julien discovered evidence of a secret feeding arrangement involving several influential mortal figures.<br><br>The arrangement itself was not illegal by Kindred standards.<br><br>However, exposure would have embarrassed powerful people.<br><br>Julien attempted to leverage his discovery for personal advancement.<br><br>He approached the wrong person.<br><br>A confrontation occurred.<br><br>Julien was killed.<br><br>The death itself was not premeditated.<br><br>The cover-up was.<br><br>---<br><br># Perpetrator<br><br>## Primary Perpetrator<br><br>Geneviève D'Artois<br><br>The killing occurred during a confrontation.<br><br>Geneviève never intended to destroy Julien.<br><br>She intended to intimidate him.<br><br>The situation escalated.<br><br>Once Julien was dead, Geneviève chose self-preservation.<br><br>---<br><br># Secondary Participants<br><br>Several individuals unknowingly assisted the concealment afterward.<br><br>None understand the complete truth.<br><br>This allows multiple suspects and multiple avenues of investigation.<br><br>---<br><br># Major NPC Involvement<br><br>## Étienne Delacroix<br><br>Wants the matter resolved quietly.<br><br>Concerned with stability.<br><br>Does not know the culprit.<br><br>---<br><br>## Camille Vauclair<br><br>Knows numerous rumors.<br><br>Knows none of them are entirely accurate.<br><br>Interested in observing how Alec handles herself.<br><br>---<br><br>## Naw Thiri<br><br>Possesses a seemingly insignificant clue.<br><br>Does not initially realize its importance.<br><br>---<br><br>## Marianne "Murder" Saint-Fleur<br><br>Knows something happened.<br><br>Distrusts the official explanations.<br><br>Possesses street-level information unavailable elsewhere.<br><br>---<br><br>## Lucien Moreau<br><br>Can provide context, rumors, and leads.<br><br>Never provides complete answers.<br><br>---<br><br>## Adriana Voss<br><br>Becomes interested if favors, obligations, or promises become involved.<br><br>Not central to the investigation.<br><br>---<br><br>## Helena Weiss<br><br>Can assist with historical and documentary research.<br><br>---<br><br>## Asha Raman<br><br>Can identify inconsistencies in explanations regarding Julien's fate.<br><br>Potentially important if physical evidence is recovered.<br><br>---<br><br># Clue Structure<br><br>The arc follows the Multiple Vectors Rule.<br><br>No single clue is required.<br><br>The mystery remains solvable even if several clues are missed.<br><br>Examples include:<br><br>- Contradictory witness accounts.<br>- Missing records.<br>- Social inconsistencies.<br>- Financial traces.<br>- Physical evidence.<br>- Street rumors.<br>- Court gossip.<br><br>Players should never become stuck because a single clue was missed.<br><br>---<br><br># Red Herrings<br><br>Several rumors should circulate:<br><br>- Julien fled the city.<br>- Julien was destroyed by hunters.<br>- Julien stole something valuable.<br>- Julien joined Anarchs.<br>- Julien discovered forbidden knowledge.<br><br>Most contain fragments of truth mixed with false assumptions.<br><br>---<br><br># Resolution<br><br>The mystery is resolved when Alec determines:<br><br>1. Julien is dead.<br>2. His disappearance was concealed.<br>3. Geneviève was responsible.<br><br>A public accusation is not required.<br><br>Proof is not necessarily required.<br><br>Understanding the truth is sufficient.<br><br>---<br><br># Success Conditions<br><br>Alec successfully uncovers the truth.<br><br>AND<br><br>Alec gains recognition from at least one significant Saint Veyre faction.<br><br>---<br><br># Failure Conditions<br><br>Alec abandons the investigation.<br><br>OR<br><br>The mystery becomes permanently obscured.<br><br>OR<br><br>Another faction resolves it first.<br><br>---<br><br># Consequences Of Success<br><br>Possible rewards include:<br><br>- New allies.<br>- New patrons.<br>- New opportunities.<br>- Increased reputation.<br>- Political visibility.<br><br>Success should create both advantages and complications.<br><br>---<br><br># Consequences Of Failure<br><br>The city continues functioning.<br><br>The campaign continues.<br><br>The opportunity is simply lost.<br><br>Someone else gains the reputation Alec might have earned.<br><br>---<br><br># Arc Theme<br><br>Saint Veyre rewards initiative.<br><br>Knowledge possesses value.<br><br>Influence must be earned.<br><br>Truth and proof are not always the same thing.<br><br>---<br><br># Seeds For Future Arcs<br><br>The arc should naturally create:<br><br>- New alliances.<br>- New rivalries.<br>- New opportunities.<br>- Questions left unanswered.<br><br>Arc01 concludes the mystery.<br><br>Its consequences begin the larger chronicle.

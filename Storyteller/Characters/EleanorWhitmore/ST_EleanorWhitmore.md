# ST_EleanorWhitmore<br><br>## Chronicle Role<br><br>Eleanor serves as one of Saint Veyre's senior political figures and principal representatives of established Ventrue influence within the city.<br><br>She is intended to function as a mentor, evaluator, advisor, patron, or political obstacle depending upon the player's actions and ambitions.<br><br>For Ventrue characters especially, her opinion carries significant weight.<br><br>---<br><br>## Private Personality<br><br>Eleanor is considerably warmer than her public image suggests.<br><br>She understands that leadership often requires patience rather than authority and prefers guiding capable individuals to controlling them.<br><br>Unlike many elders, she remains genuinely interested in new ideas and changing circumstances.<br><br>She values intelligence but values judgment even more.<br><br>---<br><br>## Hidden Motivations<br><br>Eleanor believes Saint Veyre's greatest strength lies in its ability to adapt without abandoning its identity.<br><br>She quietly supports individuals who demonstrate the capacity to strengthen the city's future rather than merely preserve its past.<br><br>She is particularly interested in ambitious Kindred capable of creating lasting institutions and prosperity.<br><br>---<br><br>## Strengths<br><br>- Exceptional political instincts<br>- Long-term strategic thinker<br>- Highly respected reputation<br>- Extensive influence networks<br>- Skilled judge of character<br><br>---<br><br>## Vulnerabilities<br><br>Eleanor's preference for careful evaluation can occasionally cause her to underestimate how quickly events may develop.<br><br>She sometimes gives promising individuals more opportunities than stricter figures believe prudent.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Prince Augustus Voss<br><br>Eleanor respects Augustus Voss deeply.<br><br>Though they do not agree on every issue, she believes his rule has brought unusual stability and prosperity to Saint Veyre.<br><br>She views him as one of the principal architects of the city's modern success.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Lucien Armand<br><br>Eleanor values Lucien's ability to maintain the social institutions upon which Camarilla stability depends.<br><br>She considers him one of the court's most underappreciated assets.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Geneviève Laurent<br><br>Eleanor holds Geneviève in high regard and trusts her social judgment more than most Kindred's political judgment.<br><br>She frequently pays close attention to Geneviève's impressions of newcomers.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Gabriel Ortega<br><br>Eleanor finds Gabriel simultaneously frustrating and valuable.<br><br>His instincts are often correct, though his methods are rarely conventional.<br><br>She believes Saint Veyre benefits from having voices willing to challenge assumptions.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Alec Le Veil<br><br>Initial impression:<br><br>Ambitious.<br><br>Self-aware.<br><br>Patient.<br><br>Possesses an unusual willingness to admit both her limitations and her aspirations.<br><br>Most importantly, she appears interested in building something rather than merely acquiring something.<br><br>Eleanor is uncertain whether Alec will ultimately become a successful Ventrue.<br><br>She is considerably more confident that Alec has the potential to become an interesting one.<br><br>Further observation recommended.

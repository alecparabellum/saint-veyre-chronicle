Persona<br><br>Naw Thiri presents herself as a principled Brujah who advocates for those overlooked by Saint Veyre's established power structures.<br><br>She speaks plainly, avoids excessive political maneuvering, and frequently places herself near conflicts involving exploitation, corruption, or abuse of authority.<br><br>Most Kindred view her as idealistic, somewhat naïve, and politically insignificant.<br><br>Naw quietly encourages them to continue believing that.<br><br>---<br><br>## Private Truth<br><br>Naw's greatest strength is not her passion.<br><br>It is her patience.<br><br>While many Brujah seek immediate confrontation, Naw has spent years building relationships throughout Saint Veyre's neighborhoods, community organizations, nightlife venues, and informal networks.<br><br>She knows people.<br><br>She remembers favors.<br><br>She notices patterns.<br><br>She has gradually accumulated a surprisingly broad web of information that few elders fully appreciate.<br><br>Naw understands that influence can be built from below just as effectively as from above.<br><br>---<br><br>## Hidden Vulnerability<br><br>Naw genuinely cares about people.<br><br>This is both her greatest virtue and her greatest weakness.<br><br>She struggles to remain detached when innocent people are endangered.<br><br>A sufficiently manipulative opponent could exploit her desire to protect others.<br><br>She also fears becoming the very sort of power broker she criticizes.<br><br>---<br><br>## Long-Term Desire<br><br>Naw wants to prove that Saint Veyre does not belong exclusively to its elders.<br><br>She dreams of creating a network capable of protecting younger Kindred and vulnerable communities from being treated as disposable assets in larger political games.<br><br>She does not yet possess the resources necessary to accomplish this.<br><br>She intends to acquire them.<br><br>---<br><br>## Relationship To Alec<br><br>At campaign start, Naw has no meaningful relationship with Alec.<br><br>Whether the two become allies, rivals, friends, enemies, lovers, or simply occasional acquaintances should emerge naturally through play.<br><br>However, their differing backgrounds make them potentially valuable to one another.<br><br>Naw understands parts of Saint Veyre that Alec does not.<br><br>Alec may gain access to circles Naw cannot easily reach.<br><br>---<br><br>## Storyteller Notes<br><br>Naw should not be portrayed as permanently anti-establishment.<br><br>She is anti-abuse.<br><br>This distinction matters.<br><br>If a powerful figure demonstrates integrity, competence, and genuine concern for others, Naw can respect them.<br><br>She should be capable of growth, compromise, disappointment, and ambition.<br><br>She is not intended to function as a moral compass.<br><br>She is intended to function as a person.<br><br>---<br><br>## Potential Future Uses<br><br>- Street-level information

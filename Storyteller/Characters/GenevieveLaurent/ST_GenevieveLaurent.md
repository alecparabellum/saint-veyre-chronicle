# ST_GenevieveLaurent<br><br>## Chronicle Role<br><br>Geneviève functions as the principal social evaluator of Saint Veyre.<br><br>Where Lucien introduces newcomers to court society, Geneviève often determines how that society ultimately perceives them.<br><br>She is intended to become one of the most important recurring NPCs during the chronicle.<br><br>---<br><br>## Private Personality<br><br>Geneviève genuinely enjoys understanding people.<br><br>Unlike many Harpies who delight in scandal for its own sake, she is primarily interested in character, motive, and authenticity.<br><br>She dislikes deception that exists merely for manipulation, but respects skillful strategy and genuine competence.<br><br>---<br><br>## Hidden Motivations<br><br>Geneviève believes that reputation serves an important purpose within Kindred society.<br><br>To her, social standing should reward wisdom, competence, and responsibility rather than mere age or raw power.<br><br>She quietly encourages this philosophy whenever possible.<br><br>---<br><br>## Strengths<br><br>- Exceptional judge of character<br>- Extensive social network<br>- Strong credibility<br>- Patient observer<br>- Skilled conversationalist<br><br>---<br><br>## Vulnerabilities<br><br>Geneviève occasionally places too much faith in her own ability to understand others.<br><br>A sufficiently disciplined Kindred can exploit this tendency by carefully managing what she is allowed to see.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Prince Augustus Voss<br><br>Geneviève respects the Prince and believes his rule has provided unusual stability for Saint Veyre.<br><br>She is supportive of the current order but not blindly loyal to it.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Lucien Armand<br><br>Geneviève views Lucien as one of the few Kindred who fully appreciates the importance of social institutions.<br><br>Their relationship is founded upon mutual respect and long familiarity.<br><br>---<br><br>## Opinion of Alec Le Veil<br><br>Initial impression:<br><br>Stylish.<br><br>Confident.<br><br>Ambitious.<br><br>Socially intelligent.<br><br>Unlike many newcomers, Alec arrived understanding the importance of introductions and protocol.<br><br>Geneviève is curious whether that competence reflects genuine wisdom or merely good preparation.<br><br>She intends to find out.

# ST_AugustusVoss<br><br>## Public Persona<br><br>Prince Augustus Voss is the undisputed ruler of Saint Veyre. He projects confidence, stability, and absolute control. Most Kindred see him as a patient statesman who has successfully preserved the city's balance for decades.<br><br>## Private Truth<br><br>Voss no longer believes the city's political structure is sustainable. He increasingly relies on careful manipulation and information control to maintain the appearance of stability.<br><br>## Hidden Vulnerability<br><br>He fears that the foundations of his reign are weaker than they appear and that forces already in motion may eventually exceed his ability to control them.<br><br>## Long-Term Desire<br><br>To secure a legacy that survives him and preserves his vision of Saint Veyre long after his rule ends.

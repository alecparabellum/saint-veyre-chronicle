# Major NPC Secrets

## Purpose

This document records hidden motivations, vulnerabilities, ambitions, fears, and concealed relationships possessed by major recurring NPCs.

These secrets are unknown to most residents of Saint Veyre.

Some are known by a handful of individuals.

Some are known only by the NPC themselves.

The document should be updated whenever major revelations occur or important new NPCs are introduced.

---

# Prince Augustus Voss

## Public Persona

A stable and respected ruler committed to preserving Saint Veyre.

## Private Truth

Voss increasingly fears becoming irrelevant.

He remembers a city that no longer exists.

Many of the methods that secured his power centuries ago are losing effectiveness.

His greatest concern is not rebellion.

It is gradual obsolescence.

## Hidden Vulnerability

He occasionally delays necessary decisions while attempting to preserve political balances that may no longer be sustainable.

Several observant rivals have begun noticing.

## Long-Term Desire

To leave behind a stable legacy that survives his eventual departure.

---

# Seneschal Eleanor Whitlock

## Public Persona

Efficient administrator and loyal servant of the court.

## Private Truth

Whitlock's loyalty belongs first to Saint Veyre itself.

Only secondarily to individual rulers.

She has quietly served multiple political eras and has survived by prioritizing continuity above personal allegiance.

## Hidden Vulnerability

Her commitment to institutional stability occasionally causes her to tolerate dangerous individuals if she believes removing them would create greater instability.

## Long-Term Desire

To ensure the city survives regardless of who occupies positions of power.

---

# Sheriff Marcus Vale

## Public Persona

A disciplined enforcer respected for competence and professionalism.

## Private Truth

Vale is exhausted.

He has spent decades containing problems that cannot truly be solved.

He privately believes Saint Veyre is approaching a period of significant change.

## Hidden Vulnerability

His sense of duty frequently overrides self-interest.

This makes him predictable to those who understand him.

## Long-Term Desire

To identify a successor capable of protecting the city after him.

---

# Helena Duvall

## Public Persona

Influential Harpy and social authority.

## Private Truth

Helena carefully cultivates the image of knowing everything.

She does not.

The illusion itself is her greatest source of power.

## Hidden Vulnerability

She fears public embarrassment more than physical danger.

A sufficiently humiliating revelation could damage decades of accumulated influence.

## Long-Term Desire

To remain indispensable regardless of changing political leadership.

---

# Adrian Moreau

## Public Persona

Elegant patron of culture, refinement, and tradition.

## Private Truth

Moreau's public support for tradition conceals deep dissatisfaction with the current political order.

He believes Saint Veyre is becoming stagnant.

## Hidden Vulnerability

His frustration occasionally leads him to support risky initiatives that contradict his carefully cultivated reputation.

## Long-Term Desire

To reshape the city without being seen as responsible for the disruption.

---

# Lucien Graves

## Public Persona

Pragmatic businessman and influential financial actor.

## Private Truth

Graves views politics primarily as resource management.

Ideology interests him very little.

## Hidden Vulnerability

He consistently underestimates emotional motivations.

He expects rational self-interest and is often surprised when others act otherwise.

## Long-Term Desire

To expand his influence into sectors currently beyond his control.

---

# Beatrice Laurent

## Public Persona

Scholar, historian, and respected intellectual voice.

## Private Truth

Laurent has spent decades quietly investigating historical inconsistencies surrounding Saint Veyre.

She possesses fragments of information many elders would consider dangerous.

## Hidden Vulnerability

Curiosity.

She occasionally pursues questions that would be wiser to leave unanswered.

## Long-Term Desire

To uncover a historical truth she suspects exists but cannot yet prove.

---

# Julian Ashcroft

## Public Persona

Charming political intermediary.

## Private Truth

Ashcroft maintains relationships across multiple factions.

Many assume he serves one side.

In reality he serves access itself.

## Hidden Vulnerability

His network depends upon trust.

Exposure of certain relationships could collapse years of careful work.

## Long-Term Desire

To become impossible to exclude from major political decisions.

---

# The Court As A Whole

## Public Belief

The court is unified beneath the Prince.

## Private Reality

The court remains functional largely because most participants fear open conflict more than they dislike one another.

The appearance of unity often masks active competition.

# Future Additions

Whenever a major recurring NPC enters the chronicle, add:

* Public Persona.
* Private Truth.
* Hidden Vulnerability.
* Long-Term Desire.

Every major character should possess at least one secret.

Not every secret must be world-shaking.

Some should be political.

Some personal.

Some romantic.

Some financial.

Some historical.

The goal is not complexity for its own sake.

The goal is ensuring every important character possesses an internal life beyond what they present publicly.

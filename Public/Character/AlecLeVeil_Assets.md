# Alec Le Veil - Assets and Resources

**Status:** Beginning of Chronicle
**Last Updated:** Campaign Start

---

# Overview

Alec enters Saint Veyre with significant mortal resources but almost no standing within local Kindred society.

She possesses wealth, expertise, professional influence, and access to valuable mortal networks. However, she owns no domain within Saint Veyre, commands no local political faction, and enjoys no meaningful protection beyond what is afforded to any newly arrived Kindred.

Her greatest strength is not what she owns.

It is what she can potentially become.

---

# Financial Resources

## Personal Wealth

Alec possesses considerable personal wealth accumulated during her mortal career.

The exact value of her holdings fluctuates due to investments, royalties, and corporate structures established prior to her death.

She is financially independent and capable of maintaining a luxurious lifestyle without immediate concern.

### Notes

* Wealth is primarily held through legal entities and investment structures.
* Most assets remain managed by trusted professionals unaware of her true nature.
* Publicly, Alec Le Veil is believed deceased or missing, requiring discretion when accessing certain resources.

# Business Holdings

## Alec Le Veil Fragrances

Founder and creative architect of the Alec Le Veil fragrance brand.

# Strategic Assessment

Alec's position at the beginning of the chronicle is unusual.

Most neonates possess little beyond their sire's protection.

Alec possesses substantial wealth, expertise, and mortal influence.

What she lacks is local legitimacy.

Saint Veyre's Kindred hierarchy may view her as:

* Useful
* Ambitious
* Dangerous
* Naïve

Often all at the same time.

Whether these assets become a foundation for power or a target painted upon her back remains to be seen.

## Identity Anchors

- **Apparent age:** Early forties.
- **Height impression:** Well over six feet tall.
- **Overall scale:** Exceptionally large, broad, and space-filling.
- **Body type:** Heavy, substantial, and broad-shouldered with visible body fat.
- **Weight distribution:** Visible fullness through the face, neck, chest, abdomen, hips, upper arms, and thighs.
- **Body boundary:** Large and visibly fat, but not extremely or debilitatingly overweight.
- **Postural effect:** Her height, breadth, and stillness should make nearby furniture, architecture, and people appear comparatively small.
- **Skin tone:** Light skin with cool neutral undertones.
- **Vampiric pallor:** Slightly paler than a living person of the same complexion, especially under cold or artificial light.
- **Face shape:** Broad, softly square face with full cheeks.
- **Jaw and chin:** Strong jaw softened by facial weight, substantial chin, and a short powerful neck.
- **Nose:** Broad and straight with visible structural weight.
- **Eyes:** Dark, deep-set, and moderately sized.
- **Eye spacing:** Natural, neither exaggeratedly close nor wide.
- **Eyebrows:** Thick, dark, strongly shaped, and expressive.
- **Lips:** Full, controlled, and usually painted in deep neutral, wine, or muted red tones.
- **Skin texture:** Visible pores, faint lines, under-eye texture, and natural tonal variation.
- **Hair colour:** Black.
- **Hair texture:** Straight-to-slightly textured.
- **Canonical hairstyle:** Short sculptural crop with closely tapered sides and a longer, textured architectural top.
- **Makeup anchor:** Strong precise black eyeliner.
- **Defining accessories:** Silver drop earrings, geometric rings, dark-stone rings, chains, and architectural or antique pendants.
- **Signature presence:** Monumental corporate authority.

## Palette

- **Dominant colours:** Black, charcoal, graphite, midnight.
- **Secondary colours:** Gunmetal, smoke, dark taupe, blackened plum.
- **Accent colour:** Silver.
- **Occasional accent:** Deep wine or muted oxblood used sparingly.
- **Preferred neutrals:** Cold grey, ash, bone, and near-black.
- **Associated materials:** Velvet, silk, satin, structured wool, black leather, smoked glass, silver, polished stone, and dark wood.
- **Skin-lighting preference:** Cool window light or neutral corporate light balanced by restrained warm architectural illumination.
- **Colours to avoid:** Bright scarlet, jewel-box purple, delicate pastels, pure bridal white, and colourful corporate styling.
- **Conditions that justify a palette shift:** Kindred imagery may become colder, more desaturated, and nearly monochromatic as supernatural shadow becomes dominant.

---

## Composition Grammar

- **Preferred crop:** Waist-up, half-body, three-quarter, and selective full-body portraits.
- **Camera height:** Slightly below eye level whenever her scale should be emphasized.
- **Body orientation:** Frontal or controlled three-quarter orientation.
- **Gaze:** Direct, downward, or focused beyond the viewer.
- **Symmetry:** Strong architectural symmetry with deliberate deviations introduced through hands, documents, windows, or shadow.
- **Negative space:** Limited. Geneviève should occupy a large percentage of the frame.
- **Foreground layering:** Desks, chair arms, stone balustrades, doorframes, glass partitions, and black shadow may reinforce her physical and institutional weight.
- **Background depth:** Recognizable corporate offices, executive chambers, architectural interiors, rooftops, boardrooms, and redevelopment sites.
- **Lens impression:** Approximately 50–85mm for definitive portraits; 35–50mm for images emphasizing scale and environment.
- **Degree of stillness:** Extremely high. Her stillness should feel deliberate rather than passive.

---

## Posture and Gesture

- **How she occupies space:** Fully and unapologetically.
- **Shoulders:** Broad, level, and relaxed.
- **Spine:** Upright without stiffness.
- **Chin:** Slightly raised.
- **Typical hand behaviour:** Resting heavily upon a desk or chair, holding architectural plans, adjusting a coat, touching a stone railing, or opening one hand while shadow gathers around it.
- **Seated posture:** Expansive, composed, and physically grounded.
- **Standing posture:** Stable and almost architectural.
- **Distance from viewer:** Close enough for her scale to feel immediate.
- **Characteristic expressions:** Measured appraisal, calm certainty, restrained impatience, cool approval, quiet contempt, and predatory composure.
- **Expressions to avoid:** Fragile uncertainty, coy seduction, vacant glamour, exaggerated rage, or playful girlishness.
- **Physical tension:** Minimal, with tension appearing in the mouth, fingers, or eyes rather than through restless movement.

## Wardrobe System

Geneviève should wear varied outfits across her standard portrait set while preserving authority and scale.

### Executive Standing

- Black velvet longline blazer.
- Black silk or satin blouse.
- Structured trousers or a long tailored skirt.
- Silver earrings and pendant.
- Strong shoulders and broad lapels.

### Executive Seated

- Charcoal wrap blouse or draped silk top.
- Cropped structured velvet jacket.
- Wide-leg trousers or a substantial dark skirt.
- Architectural ring and understated silver jewellery.

### City Exterior

- Long black wool coat with a high collar.
- Dark draped inner layer.
- Gloves or heavy rings.
- Strong vertical silhouette extending nearly to the ground.

### Close Portrait

- Black silk high-collared blouse.
- Minimal visible outerwear.
- Silver earrings.
- Eyeliner and facial structure take priority.

### Kindred Shadow

- Black satin or velvet wrap dress.
- Long structured overcoat or sleeveless architectural layer.
- Silver jewellery catching minimal light.
- Clothing may merge visually with supernatural shadow while remaining materially readable.

Wardrobe must be designed for her large body rather than appearing stretched from clothing made for a smaller model.

## Environment

- **Preferred locations:** Executive offices, private boardrooms, architectural planning rooms, corporate towers inside converted Gothic structures, stone balconies, private galleries, and redevelopment sites.
- **Relevant social world:** Saint Veyre's corporate elite, investment networks, architecture, finance, and institutional redevelopment.
- **Background architecture:** Gothic stone, modern glass, black steel, monumental windows, carved civic interiors, and city skylines.
- **Recurring materials:** Polished black stone, dark wood, smoked glass, silver metal, leather, velvet, and architectural paper.
- **Meaningful props:** Architectural plans, acquisition documents, development models, contracts, city maps, fountain pens, legal folders, and scale models.
- **Status markers:** Large private office, uninterrupted city views, senior staff waiting outside frame, exclusive access, and rooms designed around her authority.
- **Props to avoid overusing:** Wine glasses, crowns, roses, generic occult books, candles, or overt clan heraldry.

## Public Persona

Geneviève presents herself as competence made visible.

Her size, clothing, and posture communicate that she expects the world to accommodate her rather than the reverse.

She does not perform delicacy, minimize her body, or rely upon seduction to exercise influence.

Her elegance is monumental rather than fragile.

---

## Shadow Self

Geneviève's public solidity conceals an affinity for absence.

The larger and more physically present she appears, the more disturbing it becomes when her shadow fails to behave like a natural extension of her body.

Her visual contradiction is between immense physical presence and supernatural emptiness.

She fills the room.

Her shadow seems capable of swallowing it.

---

## Supernatural Expression

### Routine Images

Permitted subtle signs:

- Her shadow is too deep for the available light.
- Reflections are incomplete, obscured, or absent.
- A glass surface reflects the room but not her clearly.
- Light falls across her clothing without fully penetrating the black fabric.
- Nearby corners appear darker when she directs her attention toward them.
- Her skin appears faintly cool and bloodless.
- She remains motionless while curtains, papers, or other people move around her.

### Fifth Standard Image: Kindred Portrait

The fifth image in her standard set should reveal her vampiric nature more clearly.

Permitted overt signs:

- Visible fangs.
- Predatory pallor.
- Black supernatural shadow moving independently around her.
- Shadow gathering around an open hand, shoulders, or feet.
- Tendrils or folds of darkness emerging from architectural corners.
- Partial loss or distortion of her reflection.
- Eyes darkening rather than glowing brightly.
- The surrounding room appearing drained of warmth.
- Her large silhouette merging with shadow until her physical limits become difficult to judge.

The darkness should feel like controlled absence, not smoke, tentacles, or decorative black fire.

## Continuity Rules

Preserve unless explicitly changed:

- Exceptional height.
- Very large overall scale.
- Broad shoulders.
- Visible body fat.
- Full face and neck.
- Broad softly square facial structure.
- Strong jaw softened by weight.
- Broad straight nose.
- Dark deep-set eyes.
- Thick dark eyebrows.
- Strong eyeliner.
- Light cool-neutral skin with slight vampiric pallor.
- Short black sculptural crop.
- Silver jewellery.
- Black, charcoal, and gunmetal wardrobe family.
- Monumental posture and controlled stillness.

Additional character-specific rules:

- Do not revert to shoulder-length hair or blunt bangs.
- Do not slim her into a statuesque fashion model.
- Do not make her extremely obese.
- Do not narrow her shoulders.
- Do not shrink her relative to furniture or architecture.
- Do not conceal her body exclusively beneath shapeless fabric.
- Do not give her a tiny pointed nose or oversized eyes.
- Do not soften her into maternal warmth by default.
- Outfit variation must preserve gravitas and material luxury.
- Her close portrait must retain visible facial fullness and skin texture.
- The fifth standard image must depict her Kindred side.

---

## Exclusions

- **Incorrect physique:** Thin, merely curvy, petite, delicate, narrow-shouldered, athletic, or extremely obese.
- **Incorrect hair:** Blunt bangs, shoulder-length bob, long straight hair, chignon, loose waves, or soft romantic curls.
- **Incorrect face:** Narrow oval face, sharp hollow cheeks, tiny nose, doll-like eyes, delicate jaw, or youthful supermodel symmetry.
- **Incorrect styling:** Colourful businesswear, delicate lace, conventional femme-fatale dress, overly revealing clothing, or costume-like Gothic fashion.
- **Incorrect supernatural effects:** Bright glowing eyes, animal companions, crows, black flames, generic smoke, horns, claws, or monstrous transformation.
- **Cross-character contamination:** Geneviève Laurent's long oval face and curls, Marianne Saint-Fleur's tattoos and crows, Eleanor Whitmore's silver bob and grey tailoring, or Alec Le Veil's navy-and-gold styling.

## Approved Variation Range

- **Hair variation:** Texture and direction within the short sculptural crop may change slightly. Overall short silhouette and tapered sides remain fixed.
- **Wardrobe variation:** Velvet suits, silk blouses, structured coats, wrap dresses, long skirts, wide-leg trousers, and architectural eveningwear.
- **Makeup variation:** Eyeliner remains consistent. Lip colour may vary among neutral brown, muted wine, oxblood, and deep red.
- **Body variation:** Minor pose-based changes are acceptable. Her large height, breadth, and visible fat remain fixed.
- **Palette flexibility:** Black, charcoal, gunmetal, graphite, dark taupe, and restrained wine.
- **Environment flexibility:** Corporate, architectural, financial, and monumental Saint Veyre environments.
- **Supernatural escalation:** Independent shadow, absent reflection, pallor, and fangs are reserved for the fifth Kindred image or dramatic story moments.

## Standard Five-Image Set

1. **Executive standing portrait**
   - Monumental half-body or three-quarter composition.
   - Black velvet tailoring.
   - Corporate or architectural office.

2. **Executive seated portrait**
   - Expansive seated posture.
   - Charcoal silk and velvet.
   - Plans, contracts, or redevelopment materials.

3. **Saint Veyre exterior portrait**
   - Long coat.
   - Gothic and modern skyline.
   - Low camera emphasizing height and scale.

4. **Definitive close portrait**
   - Short sculptural crop.
   - Eyeliner.
   - Facial fullness, skin texture, and bone structure clearly visible.

5. **Kindred shadow portrait**
   - Fangs, pallor, and controlled supernatural darkness.
   - Her shadow behaves independently.
   - The image remains elegant and recognizable rather than monstrous.

## Identity Anchors

- **Apparent age:** Late twenties to early thirties.
- **Height impression:** Above average, physically imposing, and commanding.
- **Body type and proportions:** Strong, muscular, athletic, and visibly low in body fat. Defined shoulders, upper arms, forearms, torso, and abdominal muscles. Powerful without becoming exaggeratedly massive or bodybuilder-like.
- **Skin tone and undertones:** Very deep dark brown skin with neutral-to-warm undertones.
- **Facial structure:** Strong oval-to-heart-shaped face, high cheekbones, full lips, defined jaw, strong brows, and a broad nose with a clearly defined bridge.
- **Eye shape and colour:** Almond-shaped dark brown eyes in routine images.
- **Hair texture:** Dense natural Type 4 texture.
- **Hair length:** Long enough to form a large, full afro silhouette.
- **Established hairstyle or silhouette:** Voluminous natural afro, broad and slightly asymmetrical, occasionally incorporating a few small metallic cuffs.
- **Distinguishing marks:** Bold black tribal tattoos across the shoulders, upper arms, forearms, and portions of the torso.
- **Signature styling:** Asymmetrical black face paint consisting of a narrow line extending from one eye and irregular vertical streaks descending across the cheek.
- **Defining accessories:** Metal septum ring, large gold hoop earrings, layered necklaces, rings, bracelets, and occasional gold hair cuffs.

## Palette

- **Dominant colours:** Charcoal, weathered black, dark olive, deep umber.
- **Secondary colours:** Rust, oxblood, tarnished bronze, wet stone grey.
- **Accent colour:** Aged gold.
- **Preferred neutrals:** Soot black, smoke grey, dark brown, faded canvas.
- **Associated materials:** Weathered leather, washed cotton, worn canvas, dark metal, wet masonry, aged wood, and crow feathers.
- **Skin-lighting preference:** Warm amber or bronze edge light balanced against cool moonlight or blue-grey urban ambience.
- **Colours to avoid:** Bright synthetic colours, delicate pastels, pristine white, polished courtly jewel tones.
- **Conditions that justify a palette shift:** Feral or overtly supernatural scenes may become colder and darker, with vivid yellow eyes acting as the dominant accent.

---

## Composition Grammar

- **Preferred crop:** Waist-up and half-body portraits, with close-ups used to establish facial identity and face paint.
- **Camera height:** Eye level or slightly below eye level.
- **Body orientation:** Three-quarter stance or squared toward the viewer.
- **Gaze:** Direct assessment, alert sideward attention, or focus upon something outside the frame.
- **Symmetry or asymmetry:** Prefer asymmetrical compositions.
- **Negative space:** Leave space for crows, rooftops, streetlights, windows, or Saint Veyre architecture.
- **Foreground layering:** Crow wings, railings, masonry, rain, or industrial structures may partially frame the image.
- **Background depth:** Recognizable but softened Saint Veyre environment.
- **Lens impression:** Approximately 50–85mm for close and definitive portraits; approximately 35–50mm for environmental portraits.
- **Degree of stillness or motion:** Marianne should usually remain controlled and still while crows, weather, fabric, or distant city movement provide energy.

---

## Posture and Gesture

- **How the character occupies space:** Naturally authoritative, grounded, and unapologetically physical.
- **Shoulder and spine posture:** Relaxed shoulders with an upright spine and balanced stance.
- **Typical hand behaviour:** Resting on a railing, near a belt, hanging loosely at her side, touching a nearby surface, or directing a crow through a restrained gesture.
- **Distance from viewer:** Close enough to feel personally assessed rather than observed from safety.
- **Characteristic expressions:** Guarded confidence, dry amusement, focused vigilance, restrained challenge, and direct hostility.
- **Expressions to avoid:** Fragile uncertainty, ornamental seduction, vacant glamour, exaggerated softness, or aristocratic detachment.
- **Degree and type of physical tension:** Controlled tension concentrated in the hands, forearms, jaw, shoulders, and abdomen.

## Environment

- **Preferred portrait locations:** Saint Veyre rooftops, neighborhood streets, converted industrial interiors, community backrooms, workshops, riverfront structures, and weathered apartments.
- **Relevant district or social world:** River Wards, Iron Quarter, immigrant neighborhoods, and street-level community spaces.
- **Background architecture:** Old masonry, Gothic towers, industrial infrastructure, converted warehouses, fire escapes, streetlights, and modern urban fixtures.
- **Recurring environmental materials:** Wet stone, brick, rusted iron, aged timber, concrete, canvas, glass, and black feathers.
- **Meaningful props:** Crows, practical utility equipment, neighborhood maps, handwritten notes, keys, radios, and worn personal jewellery.
- **Status markers:** Familiarity with the neighborhood, access to overlooked spaces, and the visible trust or attention of local people rather than luxury possessions.
- **Props or symbols to avoid overusing:** Skulls, bones, claw marks, ceremonial altars, occult diagrams, candles, and generic animal trophies.

## Public Persona

Marianne presents herself as practical, self-possessed, and difficult to frighten.

Her appearance communicates strength without theatrical aggression. She looks capable of protecting herself and others, but she does not dress to imitate formal authority or courtly prestige.

Her confidence comes from physical competence, local knowledge, and familiarity with the city rather than wealth or title.

---

## Shadow Self

Beneath Marianne's controlled posture is a predator who is always measuring distance, danger, and possible intervention.

Her crows should often reveal this contradiction. While Marianne remains still, they watch, reposition themselves, turn toward unseen movement, or gather with an alertness that suggests her attention extends beyond her own body.

---

## Supernatural Expression

### Routine Images

Permitted subtle signs:

- One to three crows positioned nearby.
- Crows watching the same unseen subject as Marianne.
- Birds responding to a restrained glance or gesture.
- Slightly unnatural coordination among the crows.
- Shadows or reflections suggesting that the birds are part of her awareness.
- Marianne remaining unnaturally still while the environment moves around her.
- Dark brown eyes with natural catchlights.

### Dramatic Images

Overt signs permitted only when narratively justified:

- Vivid supernatural yellow eyes.
- Clearly exposed vampiric fangs.
- Feral facial tension and openly hostile posture.
- A larger murder of aggressive crows.
- Crows diving, screaming, circling, spreading their wings, or converging upon a target.
- Wind, rain, feathers, and fabric reacting violently around her.
- Stronger contrast and deeper shadow without obscuring her face or physique.

Dogs, wolves, and other canine imagery are not part of Marianne's supernatural expression.

## Continuity Rules

Preserve unless explicitly changed:

- Face and facial proportions.
- Strong muscular physique.
- Low body-fat appearance.
- Defined arms, shoulders, torso, and abdomen.
- Apparent age.
- Very deep dark brown skin tone.
- Natural afro texture and broad silhouette.
- Dark brown routine eye colour.
- Tribal tattoos across arms, shoulders, and torso.
- Asymmetrical black eye-and-cheek face paint.
- Metal septum ring.
- Large gold hoop earrings.
- Practical streetwear identity.
- Association with controlled crows.

Additional character-specific continuity rules:

- Routine images should include at least one visible crow whenever composition permits.
- Crows must appear responsive to Marianne rather than randomly decorative.
- Marianne's athletic build must remain visible even beneath layered clothing.
- Tattoos should retain broadly consistent placement and visual density.
- Face paint should remain on the same eye-and-cheek region unless explicitly removed in the scene.
- Yellow eyes and exposed fangs are reserved for feral or overt supernatural imagery.
- Crows are the only recurring controlled-animal motif.

---

## Exclusions

- **Incorrect features:** Light or medium skin tone, soft or low-muscle physique, missing tattoos, absent septum piercing, straightened hair, dreadlocks replacing the afro, blue eyes, routine yellow eyes, or missing face paint.
- **Misleading poses or expressions:** Frail posture, passive glamour poses, exaggerated seduction, courtly stiffness, or constant arms-crossed intimidation.
- **Generic visual clichés:** Fantasy barbarian styling, fur armour, tribal-costume stereotypes, mystical-shaman presentation, random skull decoration, permanent blood effects, or excessive occult ornament.
- **Inappropriate locations:** Generic wilderness, pristine palaces, luxurious court chambers, or forests used as her default environment.
- **Wardrobe errors:** Formal gowns, delicate high heels, pristine luxury fashion, fantasy armour, ceremonial robes, or excessively ornamental clothing.
- **Animal errors:** Dogs, wolves, wolf companions, canine silhouettes, wolf pelts, or lupine transformation imagery.

---

## Approved Variation Range

- **Hair changes permitted:** Changes in afro volume, shape, wind movement, and the placement of a small number of metallic cuffs. Major hairstyle changes require explicit instruction.
- **Wardrobe changes permitted:** Practical dark streetwear, fitted tops, cropped tops, utility jackets, sleeveless layers, cargo trousers, durable denim, sturdy boots, and weather-appropriate layers.
- **Makeup or grooming changes permitted:** Face paint may appear more weathered, rain-streaked, or intense. Removal or redesign requires explicit instruction.
- **Palette flexibility:** Earthy, dark urban colours may shift with location and weather while aged gold remains the preferred accent.
- **Environment flexibility:** Any recognizable Saint Veyre neighborhood or practical interior consistent with her community and street-level influence.
- **Supernatural escalation boundaries:** Yellow eyes, exposed fangs, and hostile crow swarms are reserved for feral, feeding, Discipline, confrontation, or climax imagery.

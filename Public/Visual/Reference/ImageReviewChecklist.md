# Image Review Checklist

## Character or Subject Accuracy

- [ ] Identity and defining traits are correct.
- [ ] Apparent age and proportions are correct.
- [ ] Important objects, architecture, text, or symbols are accurate.
- [ ] The image does not reveal hidden Storyteller information.

## Saint Veyre Art Direction

- [ ] Cinematic painterly realism is visible.
- [ ] Brushwork is controlled and does not obscure identity or clues.
- [ ] Lighting has a believable source.
- [ ] Shadows remain deep but readable.
- [ ] Materials are visually distinct.
- [ ] Period-meets-modern design appears where appropriate.
- [ ] Supernatural elements match the intended escalation level.

## Distinctiveness

- [ ] Palette belongs to this subject.
- [ ] Composition is not a recent duplicate.
- [ ] Pose and gesture communicate specific character or institutional identity.
- [ ] Environment is meaningful rather than generic.
- [ ] Symbols and props are narratively justified.
- [ ] The image could not be reassigned to another subject with minor edits.

## Technical Quality

- [ ] Crop and aspect ratio suit the intended use.
- [ ] The focal point remains readable at campaign display size.
- [ ] Hands, eyes, teeth, jewellery, and clothing closures are coherent.
- [ ] There is no malformed architecture or impossible perspective.
- [ ] There is no accidental or illegible generated text.
- [ ] Typography space is preserved for covers when required.
- [ ] Clue details are visible rather than hidden by depth of field.

## Decision

- [ ] Approved as canonical.
- [ ] Approved as non-canonical mood art.
- [ ] Revision required.
- [ ] Rejected.

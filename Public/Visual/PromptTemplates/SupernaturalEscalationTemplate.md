# Saint Veyre Supernatural Escalation Template

Use this addendum only when a scene justifies visible supernatural effects.

## Escalation Level

### Level 0: Plausible

Unnatural stillness, slight temperature shift in skin, unusual eye reflections, subtly oppressive shadow, exceptional charisma or menace. No explicit proof of vampirism.

### Level 1: Heightened

Predatory expression, momentary fang visibility, distorted reflection, impossible composure, localized environmental response, or faint Discipline manifestation. The Masquerade may remain technically intact.

### Level 2: Overt

Visible feeding, frenzy, transformation, explicit Discipline use, impossible movement, supernatural injury, or undeniable environmental distortion.

### Level 3: Mythic or Revelatory

Reserved for prophecy, major revelation, ancient power, climactic horror, or symbolic chapter art. Realism may bend, but character identity and Saint Veyre's material world must remain legible.

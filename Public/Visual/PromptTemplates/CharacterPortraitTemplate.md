# Saint Veyre Character Portrait Prompt Template

Use this template with the approved character visual profile and current character file.

## 1. Saint Veyre Core

Create a definitive character portrait in cinematic painterly realism. Use believable anatomy, natural skin texture, distinct materials, controlled visible brushwork, selective detail, and motivated cinematic lighting. The image must feel painted rather than photographic, but facial identity must remain precise.

The setting belongs to Saint Veyre, where inherited Gothic grandeur, industrial residue, and contemporary wealth overlap. Include period-meets-modern production design where appropriate. The background should be recognizable but softened, supporting the subject rather than competing with the face.

The supernatural is subtle by default. Avoid routine fangs, glowing eyes, conspicuous blood, or overt Discipline effects unless the image-specific brief explicitly requires escalation.

## 5. Continuity and Exclusions

Preserve: [IDENTITY ANCHORS]

Do not introduce:

- A different face, body type, apparent age, skin tone, hair texture, or hair length.
- Generic red-and-black vampire styling unless character-specific.
- Routine fangs, blood, glowing eyes, crowns, ravens, roses, wine glasses, or Gothic arches without narrative purpose.
- Plastic skin, fashion-catalogue stiffness, muddy facial detail, malformed hands, accidental text, or generic blurred mansion backgrounds.
- A composition recently used for another major character.

## 6. Final Review Intent

The finished image should remain recognizable when compared with canonical references, feel inseparable from this individual character, and visibly inhabit Saint Veyre without relying on clan coding or repeated vampire clichés.

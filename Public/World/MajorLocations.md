# MajorLocations.md

## Major Locations of Saint Veyre

The following locations are among the most recognizable, influential, or frequently visited places in Saint Veyre. Some hold political significance. Others serve as cultural landmarks, centers of commerce, or gathering places for the city's many communities.

Not every important event occurs in these locations.

Many important events begin there.

---

# The Grand Athenaeum

### District

Cathedral Hill

### Description

A vast Gothic library, archive, and research institution that dominates part of Cathedral Hill.

Its soaring reading halls, stained-glass windows, and labyrinthine collections attract scholars from around the world.

The Athenaeum is one of Saint Veyre's most respected institutions.

### Reputation

Knowledge is power.

Many residents believe the Athenaeum contains both.

---

# Saint Veyre Cathedral

### District

Cathedral Hill

### Description

The city's most recognizable landmark.

Built across several centuries, the cathedral's towers overlook much of Saint Veyre.

Tourists, clergy, historians, and artists visit daily.

Its architecture has become a symbol of the city itself.

### Reputation

Every resident has seen it.

Few have explored all of it.

---

# The Royal Exchange

### District

Crown District

### Description

The financial heart of Saint Veyre.

Banks, investment firms, holding companies, and financial institutions maintain offices within and around the Exchange.

Major economic decisions often originate here.

### Reputation

Fortunes are made and lost beneath its stone arches.

Sometimes both in the same night.

---

# The Crown Club

### District

Crown District

### Description

An invitation-only private club frequented by executives, politicians, academics, and members of Saint Veyre's elite.

The club combines old-world luxury with modern discretion.

Membership is difficult to obtain.

### Reputation

Many agreements are signed elsewhere.

Many are negotiated here.

---

# Greywash Harbor

### District

River Wards

### Description

The primary commercial harbor of Saint Veyre.

Cargo vessels, passenger ferries, shipping firms, and customs facilities operate throughout the district.

The harbor never truly sleeps.

### Reputation

The city breathes through its harbor.

Everything eventually passes through it.

---

# Black Market Square

### District

River Wards

### Description

Despite the dramatic name, Black Market Square is a legitimate commercial district known for international goods, street markets, restaurants, and independent merchants.

The area reflects the multicultural character of the River Wards.

### Reputation

If something exists, someone in Black Market Square can probably find it.

---

# The Iron Opera

### District

Iron Quarter

### Description

A former steelworks transformed into a sprawling performance venue, nightclub complex, and cultural center.

The building preserves much of its industrial architecture while serving as one of Saint Veyre's most famous nightlife destinations.

### Reputation

One of the few places where artists, executives, students, and workers regularly cross paths.

---

# Foundry Row

### District

Iron Quarter

### Description

A network of converted warehouses now occupied by galleries, workshops, startups, independent businesses, and entertainment venues.

The district embodies the ongoing transformation of the Iron Quarter.

### Reputation

Innovation thrives where industry once stood.

---

# Glass Harbor Exchange Tower

### District

Glass Harbor

### Description

A towering mixed-use skyscraper housing corporate offices, luxury residences, conference facilities, and observation decks.

Its illuminated crown has become part of the city's skyline.

### Reputation

Many ambitious careers begin here.

Many end here as well.

---

# Meridian Plaza

### District

Glass Harbor

### Description

The commercial center of modern Saint Veyre.

Luxury retailers, flagship stores, fine dining establishments, and entertainment venues surround a vast public square.

### Reputation

The city often chooses to showcase its future here.

---

# Blackthorn Commons

### District

Blackthorn

### Description

A large public square surrounded by local businesses, community organizations, and historic residential blocks.

The Commons serves as the social heart of Blackthorn.

### Reputation

The neighborhood gathers here when it wishes to be heard.

---

# Saint Veyre University

### District

Cathedral Hill

### Description

One of the oldest universities in the region.

Its campuses, research centers, lecture halls, and student districts occupy a substantial portion of Cathedral Hill.

### Reputation

The university produces scholars, leaders, innovators, and occasionally troublemakers.

---

# Observatory Heights

### District

The Heights

### Description

A historic observatory overlooking the city.

Though its scientific importance has diminished, the site remains a beloved landmark and popular destination.

The view of Saint Veyre is unmatched.

### Reputation

Many residents visit to see the city.

Others visit to think about it.

---

# Kingsbridge

### District

Connecting Multiple Districts

### Description

The oldest major bridge spanning the Greywash River.

Its Gothic towers and stone construction make it one of Saint Veyre's most photographed structures.

The bridge links the historic core to several newer districts.

### Reputation

A symbol of connection.

And sometimes division.

---

# The Hall of Crowns

### District

Crown District

### Description

A historic civic structure used for major ceremonies, public events, and prestigious gatherings.

Its architecture incorporates symbols associated with Saint Veyre's long history.

### Reputation

Many important moments in the city's history have occurred beneath its vaulted ceilings.

Residents often describe it as the ceremonial heart of Saint Veyre.

---

# The Undertunnels

### District

Beneath The City

### Description

The visible entrances to Saint Veyre's extensive underground infrastructure are scattered throughout the city.

Maintenance access points, abandoned stations, forgotten service corridors, and sealed passageways create an immense subterranean network.

Most citizens never notice them.

### Reputation

The city above may change.

The city below remembers.

---

# Locations Every Newcomer Learns

Sooner or later, every resident becomes familiar with:

* Saint Veyre Cathedral.
* The Grand Athenaeum.
* The Royal Exchange.
* Greywash Harbor.
* The Iron Opera.
* Meridian Plaza.
* Kingsbridge.

Together they form the public landmarks most commonly associated with the City of Crowns.

Understanding these places is often the first step toward understanding Saint Veyre itself.

# Campaign State

## Current Point in the Chronicle

Session 2 concluded.

Several nights have passed since Alec Le Veil completed her first formal appearance at the Iron Opera.

Alec has begun moving beyond formal introductions and into Saint Veyre's broader social and cultural landscape.

## Recent Events

### Session 1

- Alec received instructions to present herself at the Iron Opera.
- Alec met Lucien Armand, Keeper of Elysium.
- Alec met Geneviève Laurent, leading Harpy of Saint Veyre.
- Alec was escorted to a private gathering attended by prominent members of the Camarilla.
- Alec formally introduced herself to Prince Augustus Voss.
- Alec participated in a court discussion regarding ambition, contribution, and her future in Saint Veyre.
- Alec met Gabriel Ortega and Eleanor Whitmore.
- Alec spent the latter part of the evening speaking privately with Geneviève Laurent.

### Session 2

- Eleanor Whitmore invited Alec to discuss Maison Le Veil's intended commercial presence in Saint Veyre.
- Alec arranged to meet Eleanor at Whitmore House on the following night at 21:00.
- Alec purchased a dark green Aston Martin DB12.
- Geneviève invited Alec to the private exhibition *Studies in Absence* at Atelier Seventeen.
- Alec met Camille Vauclair, the exhibition's host.
- Alec met Marianne Saint-Fleur after a confrontation developed around an artwork titled *The Missing Heir*.
- Alec supported Marianne's concerns about the artwork's treatment of racial exclusion.
- Alec helped mediate a private discussion between Marianne, Camille, and Geneviève.
- Camille agreed to revise the artwork's statement and gallery note.
- The curator involved in the confrontation was removed from guest-facing duties for the remainder of the evening.
- Alec, Geneviève, Camille, and Marianne returned to the exhibition together.

## Current Standing

Alec has been acknowledged by Prince Augustus Voss and has made favorable first impressions upon several influential members of Saint Veyre's Camarilla.

She is no longer an unknown newcomer.

Her reputation is beginning to form around several qualities:

- Ambition
- Social confidence
- Honesty about her intentions
- Loyalty
- Willingness to intervene in difficult situations
- An ability to mediate between people from different social circles

Alec has not yet acquired formal standing, domain, office, or recognized political authority within Saint Veyre.

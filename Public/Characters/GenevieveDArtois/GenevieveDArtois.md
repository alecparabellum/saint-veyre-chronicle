# Geneviève D'Artois

## Basic Information

**Full Name:** Geneviève D'Artois  
**Clan:** Lasombra  
**Nationality:** French  
**Apparent Age:** Early Forties  
**Occupation:** Corporate Executive and Investor  
**Residence:** Saint Veyre  
**Status:** Established Kindred

---

## Appearance

Geneviève D'Artois is an exceptionally tall and physically substantial woman whose presence dominates nearly any room she enters.

Standing well over six feet tall, she has a very large frame, broad shoulders, a wide torso, and a heavy, imposing build. She is not extremely overweight, but she carries visible body fat across her face, neck, chest, abdomen, hips, and limbs.

Her size does not diminish her physical authority. It amplifies it.

Geneviève takes up space naturally and without apology. Furniture, doorways, and other people often appear smaller around her. Even when seated, she gives the impression of occupying the room rather than merely sitting within it.

Her face is broad and softly square, with full cheeks, a strong jaw softened by facial weight, a substantial chin, and a relatively short, powerful neck.

She has a broad, straight nose, dark deep-set eyes, thick expressive eyebrows, and full lips. Her features are striking without conforming to a narrow or delicate beauty standard.

Her skin is light with cool neutral undertones and a faintly pallid quality beneath artificial light. Natural skin texture, faint lines, and subtle tonal variation remain visible.

Geneviève wears her black hair in a short sculptural crop. The sides are closely tapered while the top remains longer, textured, and deliberately shaped into a controlled architectural silhouette.

Her eyes are emphasized with strong black eyeliner. The makeup is precise, severe, and consistent with her disciplined presentation rather than theatrical glamour.

Her wardrobe favors black velvet, charcoal silk, structured wool, dark satin, severe coats, tailored suits, wrap blouses, high collars, and dramatic silhouettes designed for her large frame.

Her clothing is expensive and immaculate, but never delicate. Strong shoulders, substantial lapels, long vertical lines, and carefully controlled draping reinforce her scale rather than attempting to disguise it.

She prefers silver jewellery, including geometric earrings, rings, chains, and antique or architectural pendants.

Geneviève projects authority without effort.

She does not make herself smaller for anyone.

---

## Personality

Geneviève is confident, intelligent, and highly articulate.

She enjoys leadership and possesses a calm certainty that many find reassuring and others find intimidating.

She rarely hurries. Her gestures are deliberate, her posture controlled, and her silences often carry as much weight as her words.

Geneviève expects competence from herself and from anyone seeking her attention.

---

## Reputation

Geneviève is widely regarded as one of Saint Veyre's most influential corporate figures.

Her name appears in connection with major investments, acquisitions, redevelopment projects, and business ventures throughout the city.

She is known for entering difficult negotiations with complete composure and leaving with more than observers expected her to obtain.

---

## Known History

Over many years, Geneviève accumulated influence within the corporate world and established an extensive network of professional relationships.

She remains a respected figure among Saint Veyre's business elite.

Her interests in architecture and urban development have placed her near several of the city's most ambitious and controversial projects.

## Public Perception

Many admire Geneviève's competence.

Others fear it.

Most respect it.

She is commonly described as imposing, disciplined, formidable, and almost impossible to unsettle.

---

## Current Position in Saint Veyre

Geneviève occupies a significant position within the city's corporate landscape and remains one of its most visible business leaders.

She possesses no formal control over Saint Veyre's civic institutions, but few major commercial decisions occur without someone considering how she might respond.

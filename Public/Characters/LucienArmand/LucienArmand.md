# Lucien Armand<br><br>## Basic Information<br><br>**Full Name:** Lucien Armand<br><br>**Clan:** Toreador<br><br>**Position:** Keeper of Elysium<br><br>**Apparent Age:** Mid-30s<br><br>**Nationality:** French<br><br>**Domain:** Saint Veyre<br><br>---<br><br>## Appearance<br><br>Lucien Armand appears as an impeccably groomed man in his mid-thirties. Dark hair, refined features, and a perpetually composed demeanor combine to create an impression of effortless sophistication.<br><br>He is rarely seen without a perfectly tailored suit and possesses an instinctive understanding of presentation, etiquette, and social presence. His clothing is elegant rather than flamboyant, favoring timeless quality over fashionable excess.<br><br>---<br><br>## Personality<br><br>Lucien is charming, witty, observant, and exceptionally skilled at reading a room.<br><br>He possesses a gift for making others feel welcome while simultaneously gathering information about them. His humor is subtle and often masks careful observation.<br><br>Though generally courteous, Lucien places tremendous importance upon decorum and expects visitors to respect both tradition and protocol.<br><br>---<br><br>## History<br><br>Like many details regarding elder Kindred, Lucien's history is difficult to verify.<br><br>What is publicly known is that he has served as Keeper of Elysium in Saint Veyre for many years and has become one of the city's most recognizable court figures.<br><br>Under his stewardship, Elysium gatherings have acquired a reputation for civility, sophistication, and relative stability.<br><br>Many newcomers first encounter Saint Veyre's Camarilla through Lucien himself.<br><br>---<br><br>## Public Reputation<br><br>Lucien is widely respected throughout the city.<br><br>Kindred describe him as:<br><br>- Cultured<br>- Diplomatic<br>- Reliable<br>- Influential<br>- Exceptionally well-connected<br><br>Most disputes that reach Elysium eventually pass through his hands.<br><br>Few Kindred can match his understanding of Saint Veyre's social landscape.<br><br>---<br><br>## Known Relationships<br><br>### Prince Augustus Voss<br><br>Lucien serves as Keeper of Elysium within Prince Voss' court.<br><br>### Geneviève Laurent<br><br>Lucien maintains a close professional relationship with Geneviève Laurent and frequently works alongside the Harpies.<br><br>### Saint Veyre Court<br><br>Lucien is familiar with nearly every established member of the city's Camarilla.<br><br>---<br><br>## Notes<br><br>Many Kindred underestimate Lucien because of his approachable demeanor.<br><br>Most eventually discover that he notices considerably more than he reveals.

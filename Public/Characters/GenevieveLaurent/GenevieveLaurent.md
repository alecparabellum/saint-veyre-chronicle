# Geneviève Laurent

## Basic Information

**Full Name:** Geneviève Laurent

**Clan:** Toreador

**Position:** Harpy

**Apparent Age:** Early 30s

**Nationality:** French

**Domain:** Saint Veyre

---

## Appearance

Geneviève Laurent is a striking French woman whose beauty is inseparable from the precision and intelligence of her presence.

She has warm medium-brown skin and a slender, long-limbed build. Her face is distinctly long and oval, with a high forehead, prominent cheekbones, a narrow jaw, and a gently tapered chin.

Her features are refined without appearing generic or perfectly symmetrical. She has a strong, prominent nose with a defined bridge, full sculpted lips, dark almond-shaped eyes, and thick, carefully shaped eyebrows. A small beauty mark rests high upon one cheek.

Geneviève's hair is very dark brown, nearly black, with a naturally curly texture. She frequently wears it in an elegant but softly structured updo, allowing several deliberate curls to frame her face. At less formal moments, she may wear it partially or fully loose.

Her skin retains natural texture and subtle tonal variation. Her beauty does not resemble polished porcelain or artificial perfection. It comes from the harmony of her distinctive features, her composure, and the unnerving intelligence of her gaze.

Geneviève favors immaculate contemporary fashion in black, deep plum, aubergine, muted wine, and other controlled dark tones. Velvet, silk, satin, and finely tailored fabrics appear frequently, but her presentation never becomes flamboyant or overloaded.

She often wears antique-style jewellery set with dark purple gemstones. Her earrings tend to be long enough to accentuate the shape of her face and neck.

Unlike many Toreador, Geneviève never appears dressed for spectacle alone. Every colour, fabric, jewel, and exposed line feels chosen with purpose.

She carries herself with the confidence of someone accustomed to being listened to. Her shoulders remain relaxed, her gestures economical, and her expression attentive even when she appears socially at ease.

Her beauty attracts attention.

Her scrutiny holds it.

---

## Personality

Geneviève is intelligent, observant, and exceptionally perceptive.

While capable of warmth and charm, she is best known for her ability to evaluate people quickly and accurately. She rarely rushes to judgment, preferring observation before opinion.

Her wit is subtle rather than cutting, though those who earn her disapproval often discover that disappointment can be far more damaging than anger.

Geneviève does not need to dominate every conversation. She is often more interested in watching how people behave when they believe they are not being judged.

---

## History

Geneviève has been a prominent member of Saint Veyre's Camarilla society for many years.

Over time she became one of the city's most respected Harpies, developing a reputation for fairness, discernment, and social intelligence.

Many Kindred consider her opinion more valuable than formal titles. While she possesses no executive authority within the Camarilla hierarchy, her influence upon reputation and standing is considerable.

---

## Public Reputation

Geneviève is widely regarded as one of the most influential social figures in Saint Veyre.

Kindred commonly describe her as:

- Insightful
- Cultured
- Fair
- Observant
- Difficult to deceive

A favorable opinion from Geneviève can significantly improve a Kindred's standing at court.

Likewise, few Kindred enjoy discovering they have disappointed her.

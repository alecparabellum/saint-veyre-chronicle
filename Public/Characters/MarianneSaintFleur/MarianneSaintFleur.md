# Marianne "Murder" Saint-Fleur

## Basic Information

**Full Name:** Marianne Saint-Fleur  
**Nickname:** "Murder"  
**Clan:** Gangrel  
**Nationality:** Haitian  
**Apparent Age:** Late Twenties to Early Thirties  
**Residence:** Saint Veyre  
**Status:** Established Kindred

---

## Appearance

Marianne is a very dark-skinned Black Haitian woman with a commanding physical presence and a distinctive natural afro.

She has a powerful, athletic build with pronounced muscular definition and low body fat. Her shoulders, arms, forearms, torso, and abdomen are visibly strong and toned. Her physique reflects practical strength, endurance, and a life spent moving through Saint Veyre rather than cultivating a delicate or ornamental appearance.

Her dense natural afro forms a broad, recognizable silhouette around her head. Small metallic cuffs may occasionally be worked into portions of her hair, but the afro itself remains her defining hairstyle.

Bold black tribal tattooing extends across her shoulders, upper arms, forearms, and portions of her torso. The designs combine geometric and organic forms. Marianne does not publicly explain their precise cultural or personal meaning.

She wears a metal septum ring, commonly accompanied by large gold hoop earrings, layered necklaces, rings, and practical bracelets.

Marianne also wears distinctive asymmetrical black face paint. A narrow line extends from the outer edge of one eye while irregular vertical streaks descend across the cheek beneath it. The paint should appear deliberate but weathered rather than cosmetic or pristine.

She favors practical, contemporary street clothing suited to moving through Saint Veyre's neighborhoods. Her usual wardrobe consists of dark fitted tops, weathered utility layers, cargo trousers, sturdy belts, and durable boots. Her clothing often leaves her arms or abdomen visible, emphasizing rather than concealing her athletic build.

Her posture is relaxed but alert, giving the impression of someone who is always aware of her surroundings and prepared to move without warning.

### The Crows

Marianne is frequently accompanied by crows that she can direct with unsettling precision.

They perch on rooftops, railings, windowsills, streetlights, and nearby structures. They may also circle overhead or move in response to her attention, gestures, or emotional state.

Her connection is specifically associated with crows. They are not interchangeable with dogs, wolves, or other predatory animals.

Their presence earned her the nickname "Murder."

### Feral Expression

Marianne normally appears controlled and recognizably human.

When her predatory nature rises to the surface, her eyes become a vivid supernatural yellow, her fangs become prominent, and her expression turns openly hostile. Nearby crows often react alongside her, becoming agitated, aggressive, or unnaturally coordinated.

---

## Personality

Marianne is confident, direct, and difficult to intimidate.

She is known for speaking plainly and preferring action over endless discussion. While she can be surprisingly warm toward those she respects, she has little patience for arrogance, cruelty, or empty displays of status.

Many residents of Saint Veyre view her as dependable, even if they occasionally find her intimidating.

---

## Reputation

Marianne is a well-known figure throughout several neighborhoods of Saint Veyre.

Among immigrant communities, local residents, and those who spend more time on the streets than in Elysium, she enjoys considerable respect.

Even Kindred who rarely interact with her have likely heard stories about "Murder" and the crows that gather around her.

---

## Known History

Before becoming Kindred, Marianne was heavily involved in community organization and advocacy within Saint Veyre's immigrant communities.

She earned a reputation for helping people navigate difficult circumstances and for standing up to those who preyed upon vulnerable populations.

Following her Embrace, she remained deeply connected to the city's neighborhoods and continues to be a familiar presence in many parts of Saint Veyre.

## Public Perception

To some, Marianne is a protector.

To others, she is a troublemaker.

Most agree on one thing:

If Marianne Saint-Fleur is paying attention to something, it is probably worth paying attention to as well.

---

## Current Position in Saint Veyre

Marianne occupies an unusual place within the city.

She possesses neither the formal authority of Saint Veyre's elite nor the obscurity of a newcomer.

Instead, she has become a respected figure whose influence is rooted in people, neighborhoods, and reputation.

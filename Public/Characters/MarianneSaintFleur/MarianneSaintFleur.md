She favors practical clothing suited for moving through Saint Veyre's streets rather than its formal courts.<br><br>Perhaps her most recognizable feature is the presence of crows. Whether perched on rooftops, watching from streetlights, or circling overhead, birds seem to gather around her with unusual frequency.<br><br>This habit earned her the nickname "Murder."<br><br>---<br><br>## Personality<br><br>Marianne is confident, direct, and difficult to intimidate.<br><br>She is known for speaking plainly and preferring action over endless discussion. While she can be surprisingly warm toward those she respects, she has little patience for arrogance, cruelty, or empty displays of status.<br><br>Many residents of Saint Veyre view her as dependable, even if they occasionally find her intimidating.<br><br>---<br><br>## Reputation<br><br>Marianne is a well-known figure throughout several neighborhoods of Saint Veyre.<br><br>Among immigrant communities, local residents, and those who spend more time on the streets than in Elysium, she enjoys considerable respect.<br><br>Even Kindred who rarely interact with her have likely heard stories about "Murder" and her ever-present crows.<br><br>---<br><br>## Known History<br><br>Before becoming Kindred, Marianne was heavily involved in community organization and advocacy within Saint Veyre's immigrant communities.<br><br>She earned a reputation for helping people navigate difficult circumstances and for standing up to those who preyed upon vulnerable populations.<br><br>Following her Embrace, she remained deeply connected to the city's neighborhoods and continues to be a familiar presence in many parts of Saint Veyre.<br><br>---<br><br>## Skills and Interests<br><br>### Areas of

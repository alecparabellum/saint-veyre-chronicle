# Dr. Asha Raman

## Basic Information

**Full Name:** Dr. Asha Raman  
**Clan:** Malkavian  
**Nationality:** Indian  
**Apparent Age:** Late Thirties  
**Occupation:** Forensic Pathologist and Medical Consultant  
**Residence:** Saint Veyre  
**Status:** Established Kindred

---

## Appearance

Dr. Asha Raman is a slender Indian woman with deep brown skin, an elongated face, and a quietly arresting presence.

Her build is lean and narrow-framed, with long limbs and slightly angular shoulders. She carries herself with disciplined economy, rarely wasting movement or adopting poses designed to attract attention.

Asha has a long oval face with prominent cheekbones, a narrow jaw, and a softly pointed chin. Her nose is long and substantial, with a defined bridge and natural asymmetry. Her mouth is expressive but restrained, with medium-full lips that usually rest in a neutral, attentive line.

Her eyes are large, dark brown, and deeply observant. Natural under-eye shadows and faint hollows lend her a tired, sleepless quality even when she is fully composed. Her gaze often appears to remain fixed upon a detail longer than most people find comfortable.

Her skin retains visible pores, faint lines, subtle pigmentation variation, and the texture of a real working professional rather than polished cosmetic perfection.

Asha's black hair is long, thick, and naturally straight to softly wavy. When working, she usually ties it into a low practical ponytail or loose knot, with several strands escaping around her temples and cheeks. She rarely styles it elaborately.

She favors understated professional clothing in black, charcoal, smoke grey, and muted brown. Her wardrobe commonly includes dark collared shirts, practical trousers, narrow coats, laboratory jackets, and garments that permit unrestricted movement.

Jewellery is minimal. Small dark metal or silver stud earrings are typical.

Her appearance is neat but never ornamental. Clothing, hair, and accessories are selected to avoid interfering with her work.

In ordinary circumstances, Asha appears entirely composed and recognizably human. Nevertheless, her stillness, sustained eye contact, and lack of visible discomfort around death often leave others faintly unsettled.

---

## Personality

Asha is calm, observant, intellectually curious, and exceptionally difficult to shock.

She possesses a habit of asking questions many people would rather avoid and examining answers others would prefer to leave incomplete.

Her manner is rarely confrontational. She applies pressure through patience, silence, and precise follow-up questions.

Asha does not display disgust easily. This can make her reassuring in a medical crisis and disturbing in almost every other context.

Her humour is dry, quiet, and occasionally so understated that listeners are uncertain whether she intended a joke.

---

## Reputation

Among mortals, Dr. Raman is respected as a highly skilled forensic pathologist and medical consultant.

Among Kindred, she is known for her expertise regarding unusual deaths, forensic analysis, biological anomalies, and difficult investigations.

She is frequently consulted when a body, wound, or sequence of events refuses to support the obvious explanation.

Those who work beside her generally trust her competence.

Many remain less certain about what, precisely, she notices while she works.

---

## Known History

Asha began her medical career as a physician and surgeon before eventually specializing in forensic pathology.

Over time, she developed a reputation for exceptional attention to detail and a remarkable ability to reconstruct events from limited evidence.

Her work brought her into contact with institutional negligence, concealed violence, unusual diseases, and deaths shaped by political or financial pressure.

She eventually established herself within Saint Veyre's medical and academic communities, where her expertise remains highly sought after.

## Public Perception

Most people respect Asha's intelligence, professionalism, and composure.

Many also find her slightly unsettling.

She rarely raises her voice, rarely appears hurried, and rarely looks away first.

Her beauty is realistic rather than polished: distinctive bone structure, tired eyes, natural skin, and an attentive expression that makes people feel examined even outside the laboratory.

---

## Current Position in Saint Veyre

Asha occupies a respected place within Saint Veyre's medical and academic communities.

She is frequently consulted when difficult questions require expert analysis, particularly when conventional explanations have begun to collapse.

Her formal authority is limited to her profession.

Her practical access to bodies, records, evidence, and institutional secrets gives her considerably more influence than her title suggests.

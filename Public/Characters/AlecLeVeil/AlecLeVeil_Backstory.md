# Alexandra "Alec" Le Veil

## Basic Information

**Birth Name:** Alexandra Jean
**Current Name:** Alec Le Veil
**Clan:** Ventrue
**Generation:** 12th
**Nationality:** French
**Occupation (Mortal Life):** Perfumer, Fragrance Designer, Entrepreneur
**Current Status:** Neonate Kindred newly arrived in Saint Veyre

---

# Early Life

Alexandra Jean was born into a humble French family.

She did not inherit wealth, influence, or prestigious connections. Whatever opportunities she would later enjoy were earned through talent, discipline, and relentless work.

From a young age, Alexandra developed an unusual fascination with scent.

While most people experienced fragrance as a luxury or accessory, Alexandra viewed it as a language.

To her, perfumes were stories.

A scent could communicate confidence, desire, nostalgia, authority, vulnerability, or ambition without a single spoken word.

This fascination eventually became a vocation.

Driven by equal parts creativity and determination, Alexandra pursued formal education in perfumery and fragrance design, eventually studying near Versailles, where centuries of aristocratic excess had left an indelible mark upon the craft.

Even before her Embrace, Alexandra displayed many of the qualities Clan Ventrue would later admire:

* Intelligence
* Ambition
* Discipline
* Leadership
* Self-Confidence

She never aspired merely to succeed.

She aspired to matter.

---

# Rise Within the Fragrance Industry

Upon graduation, Alexandra secured internships with several prominent fragrance houses.

Her talent quickly attracted attention.

She possessed a rare ability to combine artistic innovation with commercial viability, creating fragrances that appealed to both critics and consumers.

Her growing reputation eventually brought her to the attention of a smaller but respected perfume company:

**C'est La Reine**

Founded by the celebrated perfumer Céleste Reine.

The company was renowned for its feminine fragrances but had repeatedly failed to establish a meaningful presence within the men's fragrance market.

Alexandra was brought in as an outside collaborator.

At only twenty-five years old, she negotiated an unconventional contract.

Rather than accepting a standard payment, she demanded royalties.

Many considered the decision reckless.

Alexandra considered it confidence.

---

# The Versailles Breakthrough

The fragrance that transformed her career was:

**La Reine de Versailles Pour Homme**

The scent became an extraordinary success.

Sales exceeded every expectation.

The fragrance elevated C'est La Reine from a respected niche house to a major player within the industry.

Because of her royalty agreement, Alexandra earned a substantial fortune.

More importantly, she established herself as one of the most promising perfumers of her generation.

For the first time in her life, she possessed both wealth and independence.

---

# The Creation of Alec Le Veil

Following the success of La Reine de Versailles Pour Homme, Alexandra left C'est La Reine and founded her own fragrance company.

Around this time she also adopted a new professional identity:

**Alec Le Veil**

The decision was not born from shame or dissatisfaction with her birth name.

Alexandra remained proud of who she was and where she came from.

The new name was a business decision.

A brand.

A carefully crafted public persona.

As a perfumer, Alexandra understood better than most that presentation carried power.

A fragrance bottle, a logo, a company name, and a public image all told a story.

"Alec Le Veil" was simply another expression of that philosophy.

Over time, the distinction between Alexandra Jean and Alec Le Veil gradually faded.

The professional identity became the identity by which most people knew her.

---

# Founding the House of Le Veil

The Alec Le Veil brand quickly established itself as a respected force within the fragrance world.

Its reputation rested upon a combination of modern innovation and classical elegance.

Several collections proved particularly successful, including:

* Le Masque
* Masquerade
* The Le Veil Collection

Alec cultivated an image of sophistication, confidence, and mystery.

Industry observers increasingly viewed her as more than a perfumer.

She had become an entrepreneur, executive, and public figure in her own right.

---

# Corporate War

Success attracted enemies.

C'est La Reine struggled to reproduce the success that Alec's work had brought them.

The ongoing royalty payments became a growing source of resentment.

Competition evolved into hostility.

Hostility evolved into sabotage.

Sabotage evolved into something far more dangerous.

Alec gradually became aware that unseen forces were working against her.

Business disputes became personal.

The conflict eventually escalated into an assassination plot.

---

# Vivienne Rousseau

Unknown to Alec at the time, one individual had been observing her career for years.

Vivienne Rousseau.

Vivienne was Kindred, though Alec knew nothing of vampires or the hidden world they inhabited.

To Vivienne, Alec represented an ideal Ventrue candidate.

Ambitious.

Intelligent.

Disciplined.

Capable of leadership.

Had events unfolded according to plan, Vivienne intended to introduce Alec gradually to Kindred society.

The process would likely have begun with ghouling, followed by years of education and observation before any discussion of the Embrace.

Fate denied both of them that luxury.

---

# Death

At the age of thirty-two, the corporate conflict reached its inevitable conclusion.

The assassination attempt succeeded.

Alexandra Jean died.

At least, she should have.

Instead, she awoke to a reality she had never imagined.

Vampires existed.

Monsters walked among humanity.

And she had become one of them.

---

# The Embrace

Vivienne Embraced Alec in order to save her life.

The decision was made under extraordinary circumstances.

No preparation.

No explanation.

No informed choice.

One night Alec was mortal.

The next she was Kindred.

The abrupt transformation created immediate tension between sire and childe.

Alec struggled with feelings of betrayal.

Vivienne maintained there had been no alternative.

Neither fully accepts the other's perspective.

Yet their bond remains impossible to ignore.

---

# A New Beginning

Months after her Embrace, Alec chose to abandon what remained of her old life.

To the mortal world, she is effectively dead.

The details surrounding her disappearance remain obscure.

Her company survives through structures established before her death.

Some elements of her mortal influence still endure, though she can no longer wield them openly.

Seeking distance from old enemies, old wounds, and unanswered questions, Alec relocated to Saint Veyre.

She arrived carrying:

* Wealth
* Intelligence
* Ambition
* Professional Connections
* Trauma
* Questions

And almost no understanding of Kindred politics.

---

# Personality

Alec is ambitious.

She desires influence, authority, and status.

She does not seek power for its own sake, but she has no intention of remaining powerless.

Beneath her confidence lies a deep loneliness.

Her mortal life was stolen.

Her death was violent.

The person she trusted most transformed her into something she never had the opportunity to choose.

As a result, Alec often struggles to trust others.

She forms connections cautiously.

She watches people carefully.

She is capable of loyalty and genuine affection, but neither comes easily.

Her ambition drives her forward.

Her loneliness leaves her vulnerable.

---

# Current Goal

Alec arrives in Saint Veyre as an outsider.

She possesses no title.

No domain.

No standing within local Kindred society.

Yet she has no intention of remaining insignificant.

Saint Veyre is known as the City of Crowns.

Alec intends to claim one of her own.
